# Research Idea Generator

This skill analyzes experimental results and helps generate innovative research ideas.

## Capabilities

### 1. Experimental Result Analysis
- Statistical analysis (t-tests, ANOVA, regression, etc.)
- Data visualization and interpretation
- Trend identification and pattern discovery
- Anomaly detection in results
- Comparison with baseline and prior work

### 2. Research Idea Generation
- Gap analysis from current results
- Novel methodology suggestions
- Extension of promising preliminary findings
- Cross-domain idea transfer
- Research question formulation

### 3. Innovation Framework
- Incremental innovation identification
- Breakthrough opportunity recognition
- Technical feasibility assessment
- Impact potential evaluation

## Usage

Invoke this skill when:
- User presents experimental data or results for analysis
- User asks "what can I do next?" or "what else can I explore?"
- User wants to identify research opportunities from current findings
- User needs help formulating research questions
- User wants to evaluate the novelty of their ideas

## Process

1. **Data Understanding**: Comprehend the experimental setup and variables
2. **Result Analysis**: Apply appropriate statistical methods and visualizations
3. **Pattern Recognition**: Identify significant findings and unexpected results
4. **Gap Identification**: Find unexplored aspects and limitations
5. **Idea Synthesis**: Generate concrete research directions with:
   - Clear research questions
   - Proposed methodology
   - Potential impact
   - Feasibility considerations

## Output Format

When generating research ideas, provide:
- **Idea Title**: Concise descriptor
- **Research Question**: Specific question to address
- **Methodology**: Suggested approach
- **Innovation Points**: Why this is novel
- **Potential Impact**: Expected contributions
- **Feasibility**: Challenges and requirements
